Quantum Minesweeper

What is this?
Quantum Minesweeper is a regular game of Minesweeper that relies on truly random quantum phenomena (quantum tunnelling) in order to decide where to place the mines on the board. This means that for all scientific purposes, it is empirically impossible to predict mine placement. Unless you observe the mines during play (or pop up a console and mess with the DOM,) the quantum principle of superimposed states for mines potentially applies for each square on the board.

Enjoy!
